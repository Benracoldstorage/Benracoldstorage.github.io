About Us

Benra Cold Storage is a modern and professionally managed cold storage facility located in Sangrur, Punjab. We specialize in safe and efficient storage of potatoes and fresh agricultural produce. Our 6000 MT capacity facility uses advanced temperature control systems, strict hygiene standards, and 24/7 CCTV monitoring to ensure maximum product safety and quality. We are committed to supporting farmers, traders, and agribusiness professionals by reducing post-harvest losses and maintaining market value.
